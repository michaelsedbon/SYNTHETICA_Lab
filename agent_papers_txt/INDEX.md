## Global Decline of Aegagropila linnaei (Boedeker 2010)
**File:** `Boedeker_2010_Global_Decline_Aegagropila.txt`
**Subjects:** Marimo, Conservation
**URL:** N/A

Comprehensive review of the global distribution, ecology, and conservation status of *Aegagropila linnaei* (Marimo). Documents population declines across Europe and Japan due to eutrophication, habitat destruction, and changes in water flow. Includes morphological descriptions (free-floating filaments, epilithic mats, and lake balls), growth rate data, and reproductive biology. Key relevance: baseline ecological data for the biological medium used in Cryptographic Beings.

## Circadian Regulation of Buoyancy in Marimo (Cano-Ramirez 2018)
**File:** `Cano-Ramirez_2018_Circadian_Buoyancy.txt`
**Subjects:** Marimo, Circadian Biology, Buoyancy
**URL:** N/A

Demonstrates that the buoyancy cycle of *Aegagropila linnaei* is regulated by an endogenous circadian clock, not solely by light-driven photosynthesis. Marimo continue rhythmic floating/sinking in constant light conditions, with the rhythm persisting for multiple days. Key relevance: circadian gating parameter for the EXP_003 buoyancy model.

## Marimo Machines (Phillips 2019)
**File:** `Phillips_2019_Marimo_Machines.txt`
**Subjects:** Marimo, Unconventional Computing, Bio-hybrid
**URL:** N/A

Proposes using Marimo moss balls as biological actuators in unconventional computing systems. Demonstrates light-controlled buoyancy switching and explores the use of Marimo colonies as natural binary storage elements. Key relevance: foundational paper for the Cryptographic Beings machine concept.

## MARS Rovers — Marimo Actuated Rover Systems (Phillips 2022)
**File:** `Phillips_2022_MARS_Rovers.txt`
**Subjects:** Marimo, Bio-hybrid Robotics
**URL:** N/A

Extends Marimo machines into mobile robotic systems (Marimo Actuated Rover Systems). Marimo buoyancy drives mechanical locomotion through light-controlled floating/sinking cycles. Paper explores autonomous navigation behaviours and colony-level coordination. Key relevance: advanced applications of Marimo buoyancy control relevant to EXP_002/003.

## Photoinhibition in Marimo (2023)
**File:** `Photoinhibition_Marimo_2023.txt`
**Subjects:** Marimo, Photobiology
**URL:** N/A

Investigates photoinhibition (photobleaching) effects in *Aegagropila linnaei* under high-intensity light exposure.
