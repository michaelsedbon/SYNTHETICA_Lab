# EXP_007: MyceliumBrain — Reproducing DishBrain with Fungal Networks

**Start Date:** 2026-03-06
**Status:** In progress
**Airtable Links:** None

---

## Overview

A feasibility study and future experimental programme to reproduce the DishBrain paradigm — in vitro neurons playing Pong via closed-loop electrophysiology — using *Pleurotus eryngii* (king oyster) mycelium networks instead of mammalian neurons.

## Goal

Determine whether fungal mycelium can exhibit goal-directed adaptation in a simplified game environment (Pong), leveraging the Free Energy Principle and active inference framework. If successful, this would be the first demonstration of a non-neuronal living organism "learning" to play a game.
